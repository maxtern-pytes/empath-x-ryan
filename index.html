We are a licensed U.S. freight brokerage specializing in connecting shippers with vetted carriers across the United States and Canada. Our focus is on fast response, reliable coverage, and transparent communication on every load.

Whether you're shipping a single pallet across town or a full truckload across the country, Empath Logistics provides the same level of professional service — competitive rates, vetted carriers, and a real person answering your call.
